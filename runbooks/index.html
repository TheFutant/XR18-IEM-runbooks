▲ The three rules everything else hangs on

1 · Sends are pre-fader (Post EQ). Faders and DCAs move the house and nothing else. They will never change an ear mix, no matter how far you push them.

2 · Load the scene first. Tune second. Never reload. A full recall touches everything, including preamp gain. Once you've tuned a room, a scene recall throws it away.

3 · + creates. Save overwrites the highlighted row. They sit next to each other. One of them eats your afternoon.

Numbers worth knowing cold
EON612−3 dB @ 57 Hz · −10 dB @ 48 Hz · 126 dB max SPL · 33 lb
Low E41.2 Hz. The speaker is 10 dB down there. Without a sub, it does not exist in the room.
PresetMAIN, both boxes, permanently. Never SUB — that's a fixed 100 Hz HPF and the sub crosses at 90.
Crossover90 Hz · outputs mode XOVER, not LINK · phase by ear
Gain targetPeaks −12 to −10 dBFS. Not −5. Not "yellow, kissing orange" — that's an analog instruction.
StackingBass and kick only. Dedicated low-cut + EQ band 1 LCut, same freq, Q 0.7 → 24 dB/oct.
Power~12 A all-in. Two circuits. Never share with lighting.
