BAND IEM SETUP

Get your in-ears working from your phone. About 3 minutes the first time. The app is free and you'll only ever control your own ear mix — you can't touch the mains or anyone else's ears.

Join the Wi-Fi

Phone Settings → Wi-Fi → connect to BandNet, password goraiders.

No internet on this network — that's normal, it's just for the mixer. If your phone asks “no internet, stay connected?” tap yes / keep.

3

Connect in Personal Monitoring mode

Open the app — it finds the XR18 on the network. When you set up the connection, choose Personal Monitoring, then pick your name / aux.

First launch only: if it asks for a mixer type, pick Behringer → X-Air / XR18. Or just hand it to Dan for 30 seconds — he'll set the connection once and it sticks.

4

Dial your ears

You're now on your mix only. The faders are how loud each person is in your ears. Bring yourself up first, then balance vocals, drums, and everyone else to taste.

Relax — you can't break anything. Personal Monitoring mode means your phone can only change your own ear mix. The main speakers and everyone else's ears are untouchable from your phone.

If Something's Off

App can't find the mixer? Make sure you're on BandNet (not home Wi-Fi or cell data), then close and reopen the app.
No sound in your ears? Check your belt-pack is on, buds/cable seated, pack volume up — then bring your channels up in the app.
Can't get to something / asked for a password? That's the FOH lock doing its job — you only get your own mix. Grab Dan if you're stuck.

Setup Notes — Dan Only

Personal Monitoring + FOH lock ▶

A. Personal Monitoring (per phone, free app)

  1. In the connection profile, select Personal Monitoring, then choose that member's bus. This limits the phone to its own monitor mix — can't reach FOH or other mixes by design.
  2. Want them to EQ their own ears? Enable Bus processing in global settings. Leave it off for level-only control.
  3. Optional: hide the bus master fader (global settings) to keep it dead simple.
  4. No license needed on their end — Personal Monitoring runs on the free app.

B. Insurance — password-lock the mains (on the mixer, once)

  1. In Mixing Station: Menu → Setup → Security.
  2. Enter a password (yours to keep).
  3. Protect Main LR — this locks the full “no restrictions” mode so no one can leave monitor mode into the main mix without the password.
  4. Press back to save to the XR18. Stored in the XR18's 64th snapshot — don't overwrite/delete that slot in XAIR-Edit or the lock resets.
Licensing: your one-time XR18 license covers your full-access device(s) — activatable on up to 4 devices of the same platform. Band members in Personal Monitoring don't consume it; they run the free app.
